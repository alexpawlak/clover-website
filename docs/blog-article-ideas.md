## Medium priority

Good topics, slightly lower urgency.

- **Baby changing for dads: where to go when it's only in the women's toilet** — One of the most emotionally charged topics on Mumsnet and Reddit. Huge share potential.
- **Breastfeeding in public in the UK: your rights and where to find nursing rooms** — Big topic, connects naturally to Clover's nursing room listings.
- **Best family-friendly cafes with baby changing in London** — Combines location + venue type, good for long-tail search.
- **Day out with a newborn: how to plan a nappy-change route** — Practical, shareable, soft sell for the app.
- **The best UK theme parks for families with babies** — Paultons, LEGOLAND, CBeebies Land; high interest pre-school holiday.
- **UK cinemas with baby-friendly screenings and changing rooms** — Vue, Odeon, Cineworld; parents searching before booking.

## Longer-form / opinion (later)

These need more care but could go viral on Mumsnet or get press coverage.

- **Why there's still no law forcing restaurants to have baby changing** — "Annie's Law" campaign, Parliament debate angle. Could be newsworthy.
- **Why half of UK changing tables are in the women's toilets only** — Stats-backed, shareable, emotionally resonant for dads.
- **The most family-friendly cities in the UK, ranked** — Edinburgh leads (50x national average). Data-driven, good for backlinks.
